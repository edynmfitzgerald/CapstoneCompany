About Sweet as Pie

Turning classic treats upside down since 2016, Sweet As Pie has been on the muffin top of bakeries around the country. With fresh and well produced ingredients, the Sweet As Pie team makes it our number one goal to provide new ideas and options for everyone. Food allergy? Not to worry! Here at Sweet As Pie we have many different flavors and allergen free options for any concerns you might have. Sweet As Pie was originally founded by our lead baker, Imani Caddel. Imani’s passion for treats started after dropping out of law school to start her own business with the other pillars of our sweet team, Head Decorator: Alyssa Armstrong and Pastry Chef:Jackyln Mendez, her longtime friends and coworkers.Our team takes pride in creating a comfortable environment for customers and sharing good food with the community (and maybe our sweet tooth!).
